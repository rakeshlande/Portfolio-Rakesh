Laser CAD :

Laser CAD software is specialized software designed for creating and editing designs for laser cutting and engraving. It allows users to upload 2D drawings


It is Run On Two Major Factors

1.Power:
Think of power like the strength of the laser beam. More power means the laser can cut through thicker or tougher materials, but too much power can damage delicate materials.
2.Speed:
Speed is how fast the laser moves across the material. Faster speeds can cut quickly but might not be as precise, while slower speeds are more accurate but take longer.
